Pizza

Even a novice cook can master the art of pizza with our simple step-by-step guide. Bellissimo!

Ingredients

  • Mozzarella, sliced
  • cherry tomatoes
  • fresh basil
  • grated parmesan

Directions

  1. Make the sauce: Mix the passata, basil and crushed garlic together, then season to taste. Leave to stand at room temperature while you get on with shaping the base.
  2. The dough needs to be very thin as it will rise in the oven. Lift the rounds onto two floured baking sheets.
  3. op and bake: heat the oven to 240C/220C fan/gas 8. Put another baking sheet or an upturned baking tray in the oven on the top shelf. Smooth sauce over bases with the back of a spoon.
